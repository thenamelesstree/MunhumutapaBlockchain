Transaction Batching

Transactions on ShamwariPay are processed in batches where all transactions submitted within a certain time period are grouped together and processed as a single transaction before being settled. This innovative use of transaction processing significantly reduces the operational resources required for ShamwariPay which ultimately lowers the fees paid by its users.

These low-cost transaction fees are achieved by having submitted transactions pay only a fraction of the total cost of the system's processing cost. This is as a result of dividing the cost among all submitters of transactions included in any given transaction batch.

Fee Payment

The fees ultimately paid for a transaction are determined by the fee amount attached to the transaction by the submitting account based on the fee market.

If the attached fees are equal to or greater than the least advertised fee on the market, the transaction is then processed for entry into the blockchain's distributed ledger and broadcast across the network.
