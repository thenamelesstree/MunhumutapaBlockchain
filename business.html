Munhumutapa For Business

The Munhumutapa Blockchain can be integrated into your business to digitise operations within your organization as well as a platform through which you can transact, collaborate and manage interactions with your customers and business partners. With the array of tools found on the platform, there are inumerable ways you can make Munhumutapa part of your business and your application of the technology is limited by your scope of your business needs.

Sales

The blockchain can be applied to your business' sales operations through our tools which include:

  • Digital Assets

    • Using the blockchain's digital assets system, your business can issue tokens that can be exclusively used to purchase your product or service or as accumulated redeemable coupons and rewards.

  • Private Currencies

    • A monetary currency system is also available on which you can choose to issue a private currency for your organisation which can be used in monetary payments between your business and your customers or within your organization.

  • Decentralised Store

    • As a business, you can make use of the in-built store platform that can be used to process sales transactions, management of sales and deliveries as well as tracking of sales data.

    Subscriptions

    Through the blockchain, your business can introduce, manage and track subscriptions to a particular service provided by your organisation. This is made possible by the "coins" which are found on the platform that can be used as transaction media.

    Loyalty And Rewards Programs

    A loyalty rewards program can be integrated into your business by creating tokens with which you reward your repeat customers or new ones and are redeemable or exchangable for goods or services within your business or your partners. The blockchain also comes with tools that your organization can further use to track usage, engagement, popularity and distributions of your rewards and loyalty programs.

    Promotions

    Munhumutapa can be used to launch, host and manage customer competitions and promotions. These events can make use of a combination of tools like:

  • Voting System
  • Digital Assets
  • Account Tagging
    • Winners of your competitions can be rewarded with collections of tokens, NFTs, private currencies or tickets all of which are also found directly on the platform.

    Accounts

    Accounts on the platform can be assigned tags called "properties" by other accounts for various identification purposes.

    Using this feature, your organization can digitally identify its employees and members including their roles within the group. This then creates an employee registry that can be used in the organization or by other organizations to verify if an employee is a part of your organization and their role within it.

    Digital Signatures

    With accounts on the blockchain, users can generate unique tokens for any form of digital data which is are equivalent to signatures but are superior in that they are also verifiable including the data to which they are attributed. Each signature upon verificatipn shows which account generated it and what data was signed all done offline.

    These digital signatures cannot be forged, and their data cannot be manipulated making them useful within an organization for authorizations and signatory purposes. Signatures are also not geo-restricted as they can be generated anywhere and sent via any communication and verified by the reciever(s).

    Access and Multi-signatories

    Munhumutapa accounts are secured by 12+ word passphrases and these accounts can be used as access keys to various parts of your business systems or premises. An in-built multi-signatory system also allows a distributed management system to be implemented in your organization where operations are executed only after all accounts confirm an action.

    Business Database

    Since Munhumutapa is a distributed ledger system, the platform by default keeps all data of operations executed on it by all accounts making it an ever-growing database for information and data. As a result, all data relating to your business is permanently stored and available for tracking, verification, data analysis etc. of your customers, operations, transactions and usage of products and services.

    Memberships

    For associations and clubs, Munhumutapa can be used to award, manage, track and verify memberships through the issuance of tokens or certificates to each member of the entity.

    By holding these tokens or certificates, members can prove their affiliation or membership to their peers or amy third party digitally anywhere in the world. This system also ensures memberships cannot be forged or misrepresented to any interested party thereby enhancing the integrity of the organization in question.

    Certification, Ownership and Serialization

    If an organization in a sector involving certification of goods, products, services and/or people, the Digital Certificates system can be used to electronically issue certificates to passed recipients ehich can be publicly viewed and verified by third parties to determine authenticity.

    Where high value goods are produced, a manufacturer can create non-fungible tokens as proof-of-ownership custodies that can be then used to ascertain not only single ownership but also transfers and multi-party ownership.

    Non-fungible tokens can also be used in copyrighting digital content including media, art, and publications.

    Businesses in the property and land sector can also issue ownership or lease tokens to their customers to safeguard the rights of a property owner as well as future owners of the same property.
