What Are Totems?

Totems are digital assets issued, on the Africanwealth blockchain through the Asset Exchange distributed application. Africanwealth totems created can be transfered, distributed, exchanged and managed through the platform for their relevant applications and utilities.

Digital Assets API

Totem Attributes

To issue a totem on Africanwealth, users are required to set parameters that will define and govern its use and application after creation.

Type

The type of totem determines the supply control of a given totem. For example, a non-fungible totem can not be split.

Quantity

The quantity of a totem depends on its type, and supplies are controlled accordingly.

Divisibility

Based on the decimals assigned to totems at issue, they can either be divisible to the set decimals or not at all.

Controls

Totem controls govern how each totem is transacted and utilised within the Africanwealth blockchain.

Information

Each totem is required to have a unique identifying name. Information on the utility, value or representation can also be added to the totem.

Properties

Totem properties are attributes that a totem may acquire after issuance e.g. licensing, regulatory approval etc that are relevant to the totems utilization and applications.

Applications

Totems can be used in various digital applications which include:
Proof-Of-Ownership
Title Deeds
Industry Tokens
Gaming
Proof Of Membership
Shareholding
Autonomous Organizations
Copyrights
